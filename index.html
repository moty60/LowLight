Policies

Clear expectations = better shoots and better results.

Turnaround

Standard delivery is 1–3 business days via online gallery link.

Minimum delivery

You can expect a minimum of 10 edited photos. (More may be delivered depending on the shoot.)

RAW files

RAW files are not provided. Delivered images reflect the Lowlight Studio standard and visual style.

Re-edits

Re-edits typically take 1–3 business days. Additional or stylistic re-edits may incur a fee unless a justified technical issue is identified.

Usage (personal)

Personal use is included by default. Sharing online is fine. Please avoid heavy filters that significantly change the delivered look.

Commercial usage

Commercial use is not included and requires an additional fee. If you need commercial usage (especially for product), mention it before booking.
